Here are a bunch of things to do over the summer!

  1. Have a water fight.
    Round up some friends, get some water-balloons and some water-guns, and you're all set to go!
  2. Have a pinic.
    Find a good spot, check the weather and you can start your "Pinic Paridise"!
    Good pinic foods are:
    Watermelon
    Lemonade
    Sandwiches
    And many more.
  3. Go swimming. It's TONS of fun, relaxing and good for you!(ok, maybe not so much swimming. How 'bout hours and hours of water fun?!)
